Do Not Use My Personal Information As Training Data

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants residents the right to opt out of the use of their personal information as training data for large language models (LLMs). Under the CCPA, training can mean the use of your personal information as input into statistical models that predict output that can enhance your customer experience.

  • If you browse on ShopWithUs, we may be using cookies and similar tracking technologies (we refer collectively as 'cookies') that involve the use of your personal information as training data.
  • If you interact with ShopWithUs in other ways, such as signing up for an account, we may use your personal information in other ways, such as sharing your information with third parties for their training data purposes.
  • To opt out of the use of personal information as training data on this site, please click the button below. If you want to opt out on a different device or browser-specific, you will need to opt out again from each device or browser, or clear your cookies.

Why We Use Your Data

We use your data to train large language models (LLMs) to improve your customer experience. If you opt out, your data will not be used as training data, which may limit personalized recommendations and other AI-based enhanced experiences.
